Thundr Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 11/17/2023

Introduction

This Privacy Policy applies to Thundr services (the “Platform”), including Thundr apps, websites, software, and related services accessed via any platform or device. The Platform is provided and controlled by Thundr Inc. (“Thundr”, “we”, or “us”). Our commitment is to protect and respect your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, share, and otherwise process personal information of users aged 13 and over in connection with our Platform.

Capitalized terms not defined in this Privacy Policy have meanings given in the Terms of Service.

Information Collection

Information You Provide

  • Account and profile data: Name, age, username, password, email, phone number, social media information, profile image.
  • User-generated content: Comments, photos, livestreams, videos, text, hashtags, and metadata.
  • Messages: Content and details of messages sent or received via the Platform.
  • Clipboard information: Text, images, and videos from your device’s clipboard.
  • Purchase information: Payment details and transaction history.
  • Contacts: Phone and social network contacts, with your permission.
  • Verification information: Proof of identity or age.
  • Survey and promotion data: Gender, age, preferences, and participation details.

Information From Other Sources

  • Third-party services: Information from services like Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, or Google.
  • Advertisers and partners: Data about your activities outside the Platform.
  • Affiliated entities: Information from our corporate group entities.
  • Public sources: Information from publicly available sources.

Automatically Collected Information

  • Internet or network activity: IP address, geolocation data, device identifiers, browsing history, and cookies.
  • Device Information: Device model, operating system, screen resolution, and other technical details.
  • Location Data: Approximate location information.
  • Image and Audio Information: Details of the images, videos, and audio in your User Content.
  • Metadata: Data about your User Content, like creation time and format.

Use of Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide, support, and administer the Platform.
  • Customize content and advertising.
  • Facilitate social interactions and suggestions.
  • Conduct research and analytics.
  • Ensure platform security and integrity.
  • Verify identity and age.
  • Communicate with you and enforce our Terms of Service.

Sharing of Information

We may share your information with:

  • Service Providers and Business Partners for operational and business purposes.
  • Within Our Corporate Group for support and function enablement.
  • Legal Reasons: To comply with laws or legal processes.
  • With Your Consent for purposes you agree to.

Your Rights

You have rights to access, correct, delete, and manage your information. We provide tools and processes for these rights.

Data Security and Retention

We employ measures to protect your information but cannot guarantee absolute security. We retain information as necessary for providing our services and for legal and business purposes.

Children and Teens

We provide separate policies and experiences for users under 13. We take steps to prevent the collection of children's data without consent.

Changes to this Policy

We may update this policy and will notify users of significant changes.
